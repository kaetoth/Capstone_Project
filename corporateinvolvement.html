Corporate Involvement

Thank you to all of our partners, past and present. With their generous support, Indiana University Dance Marathon enables Riley Hospital for Children to continue to provide the type of care and support that each sick or injured kid deserves: the strongest team of pediatric specialists in the state, exceptional family-centered support, and internationally-acclaimed pediatric research. Joining the IUDM family means supporting the kids at Riley while leveraging the outreach of the second largest student-run philanthropy in the country. Our organization aims to provide our partners valuable marketing and promotional opportunities throughout our 11-month DM season from our 1,092 committee members to the greater Central Indiana community—all with 501c3 tax benefits. IUDM’s corporate partnerships, both from in-kind and financial assistance, are cornerstones of our support “For The Kids”. Together, we are building dreams greater than our own. We are grateful to have your support in 2014.

FTK,

Will Tenbarge
Director of Corporate Relations
corporate.relations@iudm.org

Corporate Matching

A significant portion of Fortune 500 companies make grants to match their employees’ charitable donations to nonprofits or 501(c)3 licensed organizations like Riley Children’s Foundation. Before making your gift, check with your employer’s Human Resource department to see if your company has a matching program in place.
