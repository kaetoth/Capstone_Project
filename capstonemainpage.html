Welcome to IUDM!

At Indiana University Dance Marathon, everything that we do is “For The Kids”. Our efforts, to fundraise and bring awareness of Riley Hospital for Children in Indianapolis to our community, culminate in an annual signature celebration lasting 36 hours.

Learn more about us:

Over the course of this weekend every year, we come together as a family to challenge ourselves for the entire marathon to stay on our feet in order to demonstrate solidarity with the children at Riley who are not able to take a break from their illness. In accordance with these efforts, we welcome you to join our cause, and affectionately consider yourself to be FTK.
