For Alumni  

Welcome IUDM Alumni!

Through this page, we hope to provide our Dance Marathon Alumni with an opportunity to continue their involvement with IUDM even after they graduate! As everyone that has ever been a part of our remarkable organization knows, IUDM is a family that you join for life, not just four years. The Alumni Relations committee hopes to make that a reality for all of our Alumni. If you are an alumnus or alumna interested in reconnecting with our DM family, please fill out our Reconnect form located below. If you have any questions regarding alumni involvement in Dance Marathon, please contact Amanda Crummett, Director of Alumni Relations, at alumni.relations@iudm.org. We look forward to reuniting with you!

For the Kids,

Amanda Crummett
Director of Alumni Relations
alumni.relations@iudm.org
