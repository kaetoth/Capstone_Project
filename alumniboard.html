IUDM Alumni Board:

Mission

The mission of Indiana University Dance Marathon Alumni Board is to develop and enhance the
involvement of IU Dance Marathon Alumni and future Alumni in supporting the mission and goals.

Goals

  • Involvement:
    • To nurture relationships with current and future alumni and encourage them to get involved in the marathon through their time, talents, and/or treasure
  • Fundraising:
    • To cultivate alumni and corporate sponsors for donations to support the operations of the marathon
  • Relationship Management:
    • Long Term Planning to ensure the quality of our future marathons by continuing to develop organization goals, strategies, and measures for the long term success (5-10 years) – Endowment, etc..
    • Promote and enhance effective communications between the University, Exec Members, Riley, and its alumni
  • Training:
    • To coordinate and promote programs (aka retreats) that instill good will and pride, and provide opportunities for professional growth and social interaction

Competitive Landscape:

Required Commitment/Parameters for involvement on Board

  • Previous IUDM Experience/Involvement
  • Two Year Position
  • Be an Active Member
    • Attend Monthly Conference Calls
    • Attend at least 3 of the following: Winter Retreat, Golf Outing, Fall Retreat, and Gala
    • Attend the Marathon
    • Make progress versus your job descriptions
  • Fundraising Goal of at least $500

IUDM Alumni Board Position Descriptions:

President – Christopher Holland

  • Work with Foundation staff to plan and implement successful giving campaigns and fundraising events
  • Work with Foundation staff and hospital leadership to ensure that an engaging, compelling, and appropriate story is being told regarding how dollars are being spent
  • Plan, chair and lead all Alumni Board meetings with support from Foundation staff and the Executive Council
  • Participate in planning meetings and conference calls
  • Inspire interest and enthusiasm about Indiana University Dance Marathon amongst council members, alumni, and general donors
  • Assume final responsibility for Alumni Board activities such as marketing, e-communications, retreats, etc.
  • Participate actively and enthusiastically in the promotion of Indiana University Dance Marathon—include speaking at various events, meeting with key prospects or Riley leadership, etc.
  • Be visible and make frequent public appearances in Bloomington—during meetings, retreats, events, etc.
  • Generate ideas to share with Foundation staff and IUDM Exec Council; cultivate new donors; and encourage previous donors to increase their contribution and commitment to IUDM
  • Mentor and Work with BOM on IUDM Exec and provide support as needed and requested
