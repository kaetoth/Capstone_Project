Merchandising and Apparel  Michael Moffatt

"Merchandising and Apparel is in charge of creating and selling all of the Apparel and Accessories for IUDM. We are also responsible for hosting Runway Riley as well as manning the Apparel booth at the marathon. There's no better feeling than seeing people walking through campus wearing something that you designed or helped create. If you're creative and fun or simply think you might have some good ideas then Merchandising and Apparel is the place for you! ~M$A~CLOTHESandSHIrT~" -Michael Moffatt, Director of Merchandising and Apparel
