Get Involved!

Committee members work year-round to plan and spread awareness about IUDM and Riley Hospital. IUDM is comprised of 17 committees that allow people to become even more invested in the IUDM Family. Committee members must have previously danced in IUDM. Look out for more information about becoming a committee member after IUDM 2014.

Another option is to apply to be a subcommittee chair! Subcommittee chairs work with Directors to lead each IUDM Committee. Each chair is responsible for a subcommittee or larger role within a committee. Becoming a subcommittee chair is a great way to gain more leadership experience and continue to help IUDM grow and impact Riley Hospital
